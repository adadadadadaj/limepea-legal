LimePea Help Menu 🍋

Here's a complete list of commands and features you can use with the LimePea bot.

📋 General

  • /help — View this help page.
  • /balance [user] — View your or another user's lime balance.
  • /give [user] [amount] — Give your limes to another user.
  • /toplimes — See the top lime hoarders.
  • /daily — Claim your daily lime reward (includes streak bonus).

🎫 Tickets

  • /ticket_setup — Create a ticket panel.
  • /close — Close the current ticket.
  • /add [user] / /remove [user] — Manage members in tickets.

🧩 Roles & Welcome

  • /reactionrole_setup — Setup a reaction role message.
  • /reactionrole_remove — Remove a reaction role configuration.
  • /autorole_add — Add a role to assign on member join.
  • /level_toggle — Toggle leveling on or off.

📊 Leveling

  • /rank — Check your level and XP.
  • /leaderboard — Show top XP earners.
  • /set_level [user] [level] — Set a user’s level manually.

💸 Items & Coins

  • /gamble [amount] — Gamble your limes (10m cooldown).
  • /beg — Beg for a few coins.
  • /adventure — Go on an adventure to find limes (1h cooldown).
  • /coinflip — Flip a coin and bet on heads/tails.
  • /slots — Play a slot machine for limes.
  • /inventory — View your collected items.
  • /use [item] — Use an item from your inventory.
  • /shop — View and buy from the item shop.
  • /trade [user] [item] — Trade an item with another user.

🎮 Games

  • /tictactoe [user] — Challenge someone to a Tic Tac Toe game.
  • /ttt_resign — Resign from a current game.
  • /fortune — Get a random fortune.
  • /limefact — Receive a lime-themed fun fact.
  • /roll [sides] — Roll a dice with custom sides.
  • /rate [thing] — Get a random rating for anything.
  • /8ball [question] — Ask the magic 8-ball a yes/no question.
  • /battle [user] — Battle another user and see who wins.

⚠️ Moderation

  • /ban [user] — Ban a member.
  • /kick [user] — Kick a member.
  • /timeout [user] — Temporarily mute a member.
  • /warn [user] — Issue a warning to a member.
